FAQ

Good questions

Is AutoSwipe free?

Yes. Downloading the app, swiping, and saving cars to your Garage is completely free. Optional Pro features may be offered in-app.

Where do the listings come from?

AutoSwipe aggregates public listings from third-party automotive platforms and from sellers who list directly in the app. We don't own or sell the vehicles — every deal happens between you and the seller.

How do price-drop alerts work?

We keep an eye on the cars in your Garage. When a listing's price drops, you get an instant push notification so you can act before someone else does.

Can I sell my own car?

Absolutely. You can create a listing in minutes and put it in front of buyers who are actively swiping.

Which platforms are supported?

AutoSwipe is available for both iOS and Android.

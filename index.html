FAQ

Good questions

When is AutoSwiper launching?

We're putting the finishing touches on the app now. Join the waitlist above and we'll email you the moment it's live on the App Store and Google Play.

Is AutoSwiper free?

Yes. Downloading the app, swiping, saving cars and getting price-drop alerts will be free. An optional Pro subscription unlocks extra features, and the free version is supported by occasional ads.

Where do the listings come from?

AutoSwiper aggregates public listings from third-party automotive platforms and from sellers who list directly in the app. We don't own, sell, or inspect the vehicles — every deal happens directly between you and the seller.

How does the price meter work?

For each car we estimate a fair market value based on the make, model, year and mileage, then show where the asking price sits — from "Great price" to "Overpriced" — along with the euro difference from the average.

How do price-drop alerts work?

When you like a car it's added to your list and we keep an eye on it. If the price drops, you get an instant push notification so you can act before someone else does.

Can I sell my own car?

Absolutely. You can create a listing in minutes with photos and specs and put it in front of buyers who are actively swiping.

Which platforms are supported?

AutoSwiper is coming soon to both iOS and Android. Join the waitlist to get notified on launch day.
